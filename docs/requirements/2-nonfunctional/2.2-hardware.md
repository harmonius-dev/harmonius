# 2.2 Hardware

## 2.2.1 Bindless Resources Required

Bindless descriptor access SHALL be required on all platforms. All shader resource access SHALL go through a global descriptor heap indexed by integer offset. This eliminates per-draw descriptor binding overhead.

| Platform | Mechanism |
|---|---|
| Metal | Argument Buffers (Tier 2) |
| Vulkan | `VK_EXT_descriptor_indexing` |
| D3D12 | SM 6.6 bindless heap |

## 2.2.2 Mesh Shaders Required

Mesh shader support SHALL be a hard requirement. Initialization SHALL fail with a clear error if mesh shaders are unavailable. All geometry rendering depends on the task/mesh pipeline.

| Platform | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Metal | Metal 3+ (Apple Silicon M1+) |
| Vulkan | `VK_EXT_mesh_shader` (not core in 1.4, must be explicitly queried) |
| D3D12 | SM 6.5+, D3D12 Ultimate |

## 2.2.3 Ray Tracing Soft-Gated

Hardware ray tracing SHALL be a soft capability gate. When RT hardware is absent, RT passes are culled and non-RT fallbacks (screen-space reflections, baked GI) SHALL be used instead.

## 2.2.4 Async Compute and Transfer Queues

Dedicated async compute and transfer queues SHALL be required to enable overlapped execution of rendering, compute work, and asset uploads.

## 2.2.5 Timeline Fences

Timeline fence primitives SHALL be required on all platforms for frame synchronization and cross-queue ordering.

## 2.2.6 Sparse Textures

Sparse texture residency SHALL be required for terrain and voxel streaming features. Features depending on sparse textures SHALL be capability-gated.

## 2.2.7 64-Bit Shader Atomics

64-bit integer atomics in shaders SHALL be required for the GPU-driven vector UI pipeline. Features depending on this SHALL be capability-gated.
