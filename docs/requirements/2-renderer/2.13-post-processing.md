# 2.13 Post-Processing

## R-2.13.1 Configure Bloom

The system SHALL support bloom with configurable luminance threshold, blur chain pass count, and intensity. A threshold pass SHALL extract bright pixels, followed by a multi-pass downscale/upscale blur chain composited additively.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.1](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) bloom

## R-2.13.2 Configure Depth of Field

The system SHALL support cinematic depth of field driven by aperture, focal length, and focus distance parameters. A gather-based bokeh filter SHALL produce near and far field blur with configurable bokeh shape.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.2](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) depth of field

## R-2.13.3 Configure Motion Blur

The system SHALL support per-pixel velocity-based motion blur consuming the full-screen velocity buffer. Both camera motion and per-object motion contribution SHALL be supported with configurable shutter angle.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.3](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) motion blur

## R-2.13.4 Configure Auto Exposure

The system SHALL support automatic exposure adjustment via luminance histogram with configurable min/max EV100 bounds and temporal smoothing speed.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.4](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) auto exposure

## R-2.13.5 Configure Tonemapping and Color Grading

The system SHALL support ACES-compliant tone mapping with white balance, lift/gamma/gain for shadows/midtones/highlights, saturation, contrast, and color LUT application. HDR display output SHALL be supported.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.5](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) tonemapping and color grading

## R-2.13.6 Configure Film Grain

The system SHALL support procedural or texture-based film grain overlay with configurable intensity, size, and luminance response.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.6](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) film grain

## R-2.13.7 Configure Chromatic Aberration

The system SHALL support per-channel UV offset intensity and configurable start distance from screen center for chromatic aberration simulation.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.7](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) chromatic aberration

## R-2.13.8 Configure Lens Flare

The system SHALL support image-based lens flare from bright sources with configurable threshold, ghost count, and halo intensity.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.8](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) lens flare

## R-2.13.9 Configure Vignette

The system SHALL support radial darkening of frame edges with configurable intensity and power curve from screen center to edge.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.9](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) vignette

## R-2.13.10 Create Post-Process Materials

The system SHALL support user-defined fullscreen shader effects executed as post-process passes. Custom material graphs SHALL read scene textures (depth, stencil, G-buffer channels, velocity) and output arbitrary color transformations.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.10](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) post-process materials

## R-2.13.11 Configure Local Exposure

The system SHALL support per-region brightness adaptation via a downsampled luminance grid driving per-tile exposure compensation.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.11](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) local exposure

## R-2.13.12 Configure Panini Projection

The system SHALL support post-process pixel displacement correcting wide-FOV perspective distortion using panini projection math.

- **Features:** [F-1.4.12](../../features/1-rendering/1.4-post-processing.md) panini projection
