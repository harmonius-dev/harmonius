# 2.3 Shadows and Occlusion

## R-2.3.1 Enable Cascaded Shadow Maps

The system SHALL support cascaded shadow maps for directional lights with configurable cascade count (1–4), split scheme (logarithmic, linear, or manual), and per-cascade resolution.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.1](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) cascaded shadow maps

## R-2.3.2 Configure Shadow Atlas

The system SHALL provide a shared shadow atlas with configurable total resolution (default: 8192x8192). Individual shadow map tiles SHALL use power-of-two resolutions from 256 to 2048, assigned by light priority and screen-space contribution.

## R-2.3.3 Select Soft Shadow Quality

The system SHALL support tiered soft shadow methods: PCF (baseline), PCSS (mid tier), and RT soft shadows (high tier). Selection SHALL be explicit or auto-fallback based on hardware capabilities.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.2](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) soft shadows

## R-2.3.4 Configure Shadow Bias

The system SHALL allow per-cascade constant depth bias and slope-scaled bias configuration to eliminate self-shadowing artifacts without introducing visible light leaking.

## R-2.3.5 Select Ambient Occlusion Tier

The system SHALL support tiered ambient occlusion: SSAO (baseline), GTAO with bent normals (mid tier), and RT AO (high tier). Selection SHALL be explicit or auto-fallback.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.3](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) ambient occlusion

## R-2.3.6 Configure AO Parameters

The system SHALL allow configuring AO radius, intensity, and sample count. Parameters SHALL be adjustable at runtime without pipeline reconstruction.

## R-2.3.7 Enable Per-Light Shadows

The system SHALL support a per-light shadow enable toggle and per-entity shadow caster/receiver flags. Lights with shadows disabled SHALL skip shadow map rendering.

## R-2.3.8 Configure Occlusion Culling

The system SHALL allow toggling HZB two-phase occlusion culling per camera. The culled meshlet count SHALL be observable via diagnostic queries.

- **Features:** [F-1.1.4](../../features/1-rendering/1.1-core-rendering.md) occlusion culling

## R-2.3.9 Configure Quality Tier Fallbacks

The system SHALL support defining ordered fallback chains per rendering effect (e.g., RT shadows → PCSS → PCF). Fallback selection SHALL be driven by capability flags and frame budget.

## R-2.3.10 Enable Virtual Shadow Maps

The system SHALL support clipmap-based virtual shadow mapping providing consistent shadow detail equivalent to 16k resolution. Shadow pages SHALL be allocated on demand based on screen-space coverage.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.7](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) virtual shadow maps

## R-2.3.11 Enable Contact Shadows

The system SHALL support per-light screen-space depth ray marching for fine-scale shadow contact at geometry edges and crevices.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.8](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) contact shadows

## R-2.3.12 Enable Distance Field Shadows

The system SHALL support SDF cone-traced soft shadows extending beyond cascaded shadow map range. Shadows SHALL produce natural penumbra falloff from mesh signed distance fields.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.9](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) distance field shadows

## R-2.3.13 Enable Capsule Shadows

The system SHALL support lightweight skeletal mesh shadow approximation using physics capsule representations. Capsule shadows SHALL provide indirect area shadowing in GI-lit regions.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.10](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) capsule shadows

## R-2.3.14 Enable Order-Independent Transparency

The system SHALL support moment-based order-independent transparency (MBOIT) that correctly composites transparent geometry, fog, water, and particle effects without CPU-side depth sorting. The OIT pass SHALL accumulate per-pixel transmittance moments and resolve compositing order in a dedicated resolve pass.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.11](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) order-independent transparency

## R-2.3.15 Enable Volumetric Shadow Maps

The system SHALL support volumetric shadow maps enabling participating media (fog, clouds, volumetric effects) to cast and receive shadows from any light type. Volumetric shadow maps SHALL integrate with the froxel-based volumetric rendering system and support Fourier opacity mapping for colored volumetric shadowing.

- **Features:** [F-1.3.12](../../features/1-rendering/1.3-shadows-and-effects.md) volumetric shadow maps
