# 2.15 Hair and Character Rendering

## R-2.15.1 Configure Strand Hair

The system SHALL support individual hair strand rendering via the mesh shader pipeline with configurable strand count. Strands SHALL be shaded with the Marschner anisotropic hair BSDF providing R (reflection), TT (transmission), and TRT (double internal reflection) lobes.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.1](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) strand-based hair rendering

## R-2.15.2 Configure Card-Based Hair

The system SHALL support textured polygon strip hair with alpha-tested or alpha-blended rendering. Cards SHALL be arrangeable in layers to build up volume and directionality.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.2](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) card-based hair rendering

## R-2.15.3 Configure Hair LOD

The system SHALL support multi-tier hair LOD: full strands at close range, cards at mid range, and mesh proxy at far distance. LOD transitions SHALL be screen-size-driven with crossfade dithering.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.3](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) hair LOD system

## R-2.15.4 Configure Eye Rendering

The system SHALL support specialized eye shading with layered cornea refraction, iris parallax depth, sclera subsurface scattering, limbal ring darkening, and analytical caustic highlights.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.4](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) eye rendering

## R-2.15.5 Configure Cloth Shading

The system SHALL support a fuzz-layer fabric BSDF with fiber scattering, replacing standard specular with a fabric-appropriate lobe. Cloth-specific material parameters (fuzz color, scatter width) SHALL be configurable.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.5](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) cloth rendering

## R-2.15.6 Configure Skin Rendering

The system SHALL support screen-space subsurface profiles using the Burley normalized diffusion model. Per-material scatter radius, scatter color, and transmission distance SHALL be configurable.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.6](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) skin rendering

## R-2.15.7 Assign Deep Opacity Maps

The system SHALL support per-light deep opacity maps for strand hair self-shadowing. Opacity maps SHALL be generated during shadow passes and sampled during hair shading.

- **Features:** [F-2.3.1](../../features/2-advanced-rendering/2.3-hair-and-characters.md) strand-based hair rendering

## R-2.15.8 Select Character Shading Quality

The system SHALL support quality tier selection controlling strand count, SSS sample count, and eye detail level. Quality tiers SHALL map to predefined parameter sets.
