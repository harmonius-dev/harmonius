# User Stories -- 5.2 Spatial Audio

## US-5.2.1 Hear Sounds in 3D Space
**As a** player, **I want** sounds to move smoothly in 3D space with correct panning,
distance rolloff, and Doppler pitch shift, **so that** I can locate enemies and events by
ear without visual confirmation.

## US-5.2.2 Tune Distance Rolloff Per Sound
**As an** audio designer, **I want** configurable distance attenuation curves (inverse,
logarithmic, linear, custom) per sound source, **so that** I can balance the audibility of
distant siege weaponry against nearby footsteps without global gain compression.

## US-5.2.3 Experience Immersive Headphone Audio
**As a** player, **I want** spatialized audio rendered through HRTF binaural processing on
headphones, **so that** I perceive accurate elevation and front-back cues that stereo
panning alone cannot provide.

## US-5.2.4 Hear Muffled Sounds Through Walls
**As a** player, **I want** sounds behind walls and obstacles to be muffled based on the
material between me and the source, **so that** a wooden door and a stone wall produce
different filtering and I can judge what is between me and the sound.

## US-5.2.5 Hear Combat Echoing Through Corridors
**As a** player, **I want** sound to propagate through doorways, windows, and tunnels with
realistic delay and filtering, **so that** I hear combat echoing around corners in dungeons
and can judge how far away the action is.

## US-5.2.6 Feel Different Rooms Sound Different
**As a** player, **I want** reverb and early reflections to change smoothly as I move between
rooms (taverns, caves, open fields), **so that** each space has a distinct acoustic identity
without jarring transitions at doorways.
