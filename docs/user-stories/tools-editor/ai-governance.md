# User Stories: AI Governance and Provenance

## US-15.7.1 AI Provenance Compliance

**As a** technical director, **I want** every AI-generated asset to carry a persistent
provenance tag that survives import, cook, and packaging, **so that** the studio can comply
with platform holder policies and regional regulations requiring AI content disclosure.

## US-15.7.2 Granular AI Feature Control

**As a** technical director, **I want** independent toggles for generative AI content and
AI editor assistance, **so that** the studio can enable productivity assistance while
disabling generative content creation for legal or policy reasons.
