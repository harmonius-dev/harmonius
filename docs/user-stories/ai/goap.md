# User Stories -- 7.5 Goal-Oriented Action Planning

## US-7.5.1 NPCs Compose Plans Dynamically
**As an** AI designer, **I want** NPCs to chain actions (find weapon, equip weapon, engage
target) automatically from goal definitions, **so that** I define goals and actions
independently and the planner discovers valid sequences without hand-authored scripts.

## US-7.5.2 NPCs Replan When Plans Become Invalid
**As a** player, **I want** an NPC that loses its weapon mid-plan to replan immediately (find
a new weapon or switch to melee), **so that** NPCs adapt to unexpected situations instead of
standing idle with a broken plan.
