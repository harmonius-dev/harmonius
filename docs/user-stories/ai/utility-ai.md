# User Stories -- 7.4 Utility AI

## US-7.4.1 Tune NPC Priorities with Response Curves
**As an** AI designer, **I want** to adjust NPC decision-making by editing response curves
in a data asset (linear, logistic, step), **so that** I can make a merchant prioritize fleeing
over fighting without writing code.

## US-7.4.2 Prevent Low-Urgency Actions During Emergencies
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs to prioritize survival actions (heal, flee) over social
actions (chat, trade) when their health is low, **so that** NPCs behave believably under
pressure instead of ignoring threats.
