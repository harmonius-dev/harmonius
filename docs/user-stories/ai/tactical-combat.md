# User Stories -- 7.8 Tactical Combat AI

## US-7.8.1 Face Enemies That Flank and Suppress
**As a** player, **I want** enemy squads to flank my position and use suppressive fire to pin
me down, **so that** combat encounters feel tactically challenging and force me to reposition.

## US-7.8.2 Watch Enemies Search for Me After Breaking Contact
**As a** player, **I want** enemies to systematically search the area after I break line of
sight, checking cover points and rooms before giving up, **so that** escaping combat feels
tense and requires genuine evasion rather than simply turning a corner.
