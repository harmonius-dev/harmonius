# User Stories -- 7.2 Steering & Avoidance

## US-7.2.1 Move Through Crowds Without Overlapping
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs in a crowded city street to flow around each other without
clipping or piling up, **so that** dense areas feel like a living city rather than a broken
simulation.

## US-7.2.2 Command Units in Formation
**As an** AI designer, **I want** to assign a squad of NPCs to a wedge or column formation
that automatically adjusts spacing when terrain narrows, **so that** military patrols look
organized and respond to the environment.
