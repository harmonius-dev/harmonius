# User Stories — 13.13 Social and Guild Systems

## F-13.13.1 Guild Management System

### US-13.13.1a Guild Creation and Membership
**As a** player, **I want** to create a guild with a name and emblem, invite friends, and
disband it when no longer needed, **so that** I can form a persistent social group for
coordinated gameplay.

### US-13.13.1b Guild Rank Configuration
**As a** guild leader, **I want** to define hierarchical ranks with per-rank permissions
(invite, kick, promote, bank access), **so that** I can delegate management responsibilities
while preventing junior members from performing destructive actions.

### US-13.13.1c Guild Leveling Progression
**As a** guild member, **I want** my quest completions, dungeon clears, and PvP wins to
contribute XP toward guild leveling that unlocks perks (bonus XP, larger roster, guild mounts),
**so that** our collective activity is rewarded with tangible benefits.

### US-13.13.1d Guild Roster Browsing
**As a** guild officer, **I want** to view a roster of all guild members showing online status,
rank, level, class, and last login, **so that** I can identify inactive members and coordinate
with online guildmates.

## F-13.13.2 Guild Storage and Bank

### US-13.13.2 Shared Guild Bank Access
**As a** guild member, **I want** to deposit and withdraw items from a shared guild bank with
per-rank permissions and daily limits, **so that** the guild can pool resources while preventing
any single member from draining the bank.

## F-13.13.3 Guild Territory and Warfare

### US-13.13.3a Territory Claiming
**As a** guild leader, **I want** to claim territory by constructing a guild hall or capturing
a control point, **so that** my guild gains resource bonuses and a visible presence on the
world map.

### US-13.13.3b Declaring Guild Wars
**As a** guild officer, **I want** to formally declare war on a rival guild to enable PvP
between our members in contested zones, **so that** we can compete for territory without
affecting uninvolved players.

### US-13.13.3c Sieging Enemy Structures
**As a** guild member, **I want** to attack enemy guild structures during scheduled war
windows, **so that** we can capture their territory through coordinated siege assaults at
predictable times.

### US-13.13.3d Viewing Guild Rankings
**As a** player, **I want** to view server-wide guild leaderboards ranked by conquest points,
war wins, and territory count, **so that** I can see which guilds dominate and choose a
competitive guild to join.

## F-13.13.4 Friends List and Social Graph

### US-13.13.4 Managing Friends and Blocking Players
**As a** player, **I want** to maintain a friends list with online status, send direct
messages, block unwanted contacts, and import platform friends, **so that** I can coordinate
play with friends and avoid harassment.

## F-13.13.5 In-Game Mail System

### US-13.13.5a Sending and Receiving Mail
**As a** player, **I want** to send asynchronous mail with subject and body text to another
player by name, **so that** I can communicate with offline friends and receive a notification
when they reply.

### US-13.13.5b Mailing Items and Currency
**As a** player, **I want** to attach items and currency to mail, including COD (cash-on-
delivery) that requires payment before collection, **so that** I can trade with offline players
securely.

### US-13.13.5c Receiving Automated System Mail
**As a** player, **I want** to receive system mail for auction results, GM notices, and event
rewards with item attachments, **so that** automated game events deliver rewards reliably even
when I am offline.

## F-13.13.6 Chat Channel System

### US-13.13.6a Chatting Across Channels
**As a** player, **I want** to send and receive messages across multiple simultaneous chat
channels (global, zone, trade, party, guild, whisper, LFG), **so that** I can communicate in
the right context without messages from different purposes mixing together.

### US-13.13.6b Linking Items and Filtering Chat
**As a** player, **I want** to link items in trade chat with hover tooltips and have profanity
and spam automatically filtered, **so that** trade communication is informative and the chat
environment stays healthy.

### US-13.13.6c Creating Custom Chat Channels
**As a** raid leader, **I want** to create a password-protected custom chat channel with
moderator roles, **so that** my raid team has a private communication space I can manage.

## F-13.13.7 Emote and Social Action System

### US-13.13.7 Performing Emotes
**As a** player, **I want** to trigger character emotes via chat commands or an emote wheel,
including paired emotes with other players, **so that** I can express myself socially through
my character's animations.

## F-13.13.8 Player Inspection

### US-13.13.8 Inspecting Another Player
**As a** player, **I want** to inspect another player's gear, stats, and talents with
configurable privacy, **so that** I can evaluate group composition and admire equipment.

## F-13.13.9 Dungeon and Group Finder

### US-13.13.9 Queuing for Dungeons by Role
**As a** player, **I want** to queue for dungeon content by my role (tank, healer, DPS) and
be automatically matched with a balanced group, **so that** I can participate in group content
without spending time manually assembling a party.

## F-13.13.10 Arena and Battleground System

### US-13.13.10a Competing in Arenas
**As a** PvP player, **I want** to queue for rated 2v2, 3v3, or 5v5 arena matches from
anywhere in the world, **so that** I can engage in skill-based competitive deathmatch with
tracked rating.

### US-13.13.10b Playing Battlegrounds
**As a** PvP player, **I want** to queue for objective-based battlegrounds (CTF, point
control, payload) with balanced teams, **so that** I can enjoy varied large-scale PvP beyond
deathmatch.

### US-13.13.10c Tracking Seasonal PvP Rating
**As a** competitive player, **I want** my PvP rating tracked across seasonal leaderboards
with placement matches and exclusive end-of-season rewards, **so that** I have recurring
competitive goals and visible prestige.

### US-13.13.10d Playing with Normalized Stats
**As a** new PvP player, **I want** optional stat normalization in rated matches, **so that**
skill determines the outcome rather than gear progression, and I can compete fairly from
the start.
