# User Stories -- Racing Systems (13.22)

## US-13.22.1 Track Racing with Multiple Modes
**As a** player, **I want** to race on checkpoint-enforced tracks in multiple modes (circuit,
time trial, elimination, drift challenge), **so that** I can experience varied racing formats
with competitive scoring and progression.

## US-13.22.2 Ghost Replay and Leaderboard Competition
**As a** player, **I want** to race against a transparent ghost of my best run and compete on
per-track leaderboards with global rankings, **so that** I have tangible benchmarks for
improvement and long-term competitive motivation.

## US-13.22.3 Race Against AI That Follow Racing Lines
**As a** player, **I want** AI racers to navigate the track along realistic racing lines with
configurable braking points and speed, **so that** races feel competitive and believable.

## US-13.22.4 Race AI That Keep the Competition Close
**As a** player, **I want** rubber-banding to keep AI racers within a competitive gap of my
position, **so that** races feel exciting regardless of my skill level.

## US-13.22.5 Race Against AI With Different Personalities
**As a** player, **I want** AI racers that vary in aggressiveness (cautious, balanced,
aggressive) for collision avoidance and line defense, **so that** each AI opponent feels
unique and races are unpredictable.
