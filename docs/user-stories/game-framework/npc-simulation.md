# User Stories -- NPC Simulation (13.19)

## US-13.19.1 NPC Relationships and Social Dynamics
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs to remember my actions, form opinions based on personality
traits, and spread reputation through gossip, **so that** the social world feels alive and my
choices have cascading consequences across the NPC community.

## US-13.19.2 NPC Daily Schedules and Ambient Life
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs to follow daily routines (work, eat, sleep) that vary by day
and season, with contextual barks reflecting their state, **so that** the game world feels
inhabited by believable characters rather than static quest givers.

## US-13.19.3 Threat and Aggro Management
**As a** game developer, **I want** enemies to track threat from damage, healing, and taunts
and target the highest-threat actor, **so that** I can implement tank/healer/DPS role
differentiation in combat encounters.

## US-13.19.4 NPC Deed Memory and Reputation
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs to witness my deeds, judge them by personality, and store
memories that decay over time, **so that** my actions have lasting but not permanent social
consequences.

## US-13.19.5 Gossip Spreading Through NPC Networks
**As a** player, **I want** NPCs to share gossip about my deeds with other NPCs they interact
with, degrading in accuracy with each retelling, **so that** my reputation spreads organically
through the social network.

## US-13.19.6 Emergent Village-Wide Reputation
**As a** player, **I want** helping one NPC in a village to gradually improve my standing with
the whole village as gossip spreads, **so that** consistent good behavior is rewarded with
community-wide benefits.

## US-13.19.7 NPC Schedule Data Authored Visually
**As a** designer, **I want** to define NPC daily schedules as data tables with time ranges,
locations, activities, and day/season variation in the visual editor, **so that** I can create
diverse NPC routines without code.

## US-13.19.8 Watch NPCs Move Between Schedule Locations
**As a** player, **I want** to see NPCs walk between their schedule locations at the correct
times, **so that** the world feels alive with NPCs going about their daily routines.

## US-13.19.9 Interact With NPCs Only During Their Available Hours
**As a** player, **I want** shopkeepers to only sell during work hours and quest givers to be
unavailable while sleeping, with the UI telling me why, **so that** NPC availability feels
realistic and I can plan my activities.
