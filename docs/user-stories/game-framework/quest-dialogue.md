# User Stories — 13.6 Quest and Dialogue

## US-13.6.1 Design Branching Quest Lines Visually

**As a** designer, **I want** to build quests as visual directed acyclic graphs with typed
objectives, prerequisites, and branching paths, **so that** I can create non-linear story
content and verify quest flow without writing code.

## US-13.6.2 Track Multiple Active Quests at a Glance

**As a** player, **I want** a quest journal that categorizes my active, completed, and failed
quests with progress counters and map waypoints, **so that** I always know where to go and
what to do next.

## US-13.6.3 Feel Immersed During NPC Conversations

**As a** player, **I want** conversations with NPCs to frame the camera cinematically, duck
ambient audio, and hide HUD elements, **so that** dialogue feels like a focused, immersive
narrative moment rather than a popup menu.

## US-13.6.4 Resume a Conversation After Being Interrupted

**As a** player, **I want** to resume an interrupted conversation from where I left off when
I return to the NPC, **so that** I do not have to re-read dialogue I already saw before being
attacked or disconnected.

## US-13.6.5 Experience Server-Driven World Events

**As a** player, **I want** the game world to change through server-driven events like
invasions and seasonal festivals that all players experience simultaneously, **so that** the
world feels alive and dynamic.

## US-13.6.6 See a Personalized World Based on Quest Progress

**As a** player, **I want** zones to reflect my quest progress (e.g., a rebuilt town after
completing a quest) while other players see their own version, **so that** my actions have
visible, lasting impact on the world.
