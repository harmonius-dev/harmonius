# User Stories -- Advertising Integration (13.28)

## US-13.28.1 Watch an Ad for a Reward

**As a** free-to-play player (P-23), **I want** to watch a short video ad at the end of a dungeon
run and receive bonus loot or currency, **so that** I can progress faster without spending real
money while understanding exactly what I will receive before opting in.

## US-13.28.2 Configure Ad Placements and Frequency

**As a** game designer (P-5), **I want** to define ad placement rules (which screens show ads,
frequency caps, cooldown timers, IAP-exempt flags) in the visual editor without writing code,
**so that** I can tune monetization behavior in response to analytics without requiring an
engineer or a client update.

## US-13.28.3 Maximize Ad Revenue Across Networks

**As a** studio executive (P-1), **I want** the ad mediation layer to automatically select the
highest-paying ad network for each impression using real-time bidding or waterfall prioritization,
**so that** ad revenue is maximized without manual per-network management.

## US-13.28.4 Play Without Ad Interruptions After Purchase

**As a** player who has purchased the premium battle pass (P-23), **I want** interstitial and
banner ads to be permanently removed from my experience, **so that** my purchase feels rewarding
and I am not interrupted by ads after supporting the game financially.

## US-13.28.5 Comply with Regional Privacy Regulations

**As a** studio executive (P-1), **I want** the ad system to collect GDPR/CCPA consent before
initializing any ad network and immediately disable all ad tracking when consent is withdrawn,
**so that** the game complies with regional privacy laws and avoids regulatory penalties.
