# User Stories -- Pets, Companions, and Mounts (13.15)

## Companions

### US-13.15.1 Companion Commands and Combat
**As a** player, **I want** to issue commands to AI companions (follow, guard, assist, stay)
and have them fight alongside me using abilities, **so that** I can rely on companion
support in combat and exploration.

### US-13.15.2 Pet Needs and Mood
**As a** player, **I want** my pet's mood to affect its behavior based on how well I care
for it, **so that** the companion relationship feels meaningful and responsive.

## Mounts

### US-13.15.3a Summon and Dismiss Mounts

**As a** player, **I want** to summon mounts from a collection UI and dismiss them on
command, **so that** I can quickly access my mount roster.

### US-13.15.3b Ride with Mount-Specific Movement

**As a** player, **I want** mounting to switch my locomotion to the mount's speed and
physics, **so that** riding feels distinct from walking.

### US-13.15.3c Fight While Mounted

**As a** player, **I want** to use a restricted set of abilities while mounted, **so that**
I can fight without always dismounting.

### US-13.15.3d Ride Ground, Flying, and Aquatic Mounts

**As a** player, **I want** different mount types for ground, air, and water travel, **so
that** I can traverse any terrain with the right mount.

## Taming

### US-13.15.4 Creature Taming
**As a** player, **I want** to tame wild creatures through feeding and patience, **so that**
I can acquire new companions from the world.

## Evolution and Breeding

### US-13.15.5a Watch My Pet Grow Through Life Stages

**As a** player, **I want** my companion to grow from baby to adult with visible changes,
**so that** I feel rewarded for raising it over time.

### US-13.15.5b Evolve My Pet Through Diet and Training

**As a** player, **I want** my pet's evolution to branch based on how I raise it, **so
that** I can specialize my companion to match my playstyle.

### US-13.15.5c Breed Pets for Trait Inheritance

**As a** player, **I want** to breed two pets and see offspring inherit parental traits,
**so that** I can develop stronger companions across generations.
