# User Stories -- Traversal and Interaction (13.17)

## US-13.17.1 Context-Sensitive World Interaction
**As a** player, **I want** to see interaction prompts when facing usable objects and execute
instant, channeled, or radial-menu interactions, **so that** I can engage with the world
naturally without memorizing complex controls.

## US-13.17.2 Fluid Parkour and Climbing
**As a** player, **I want** to vault, mantle, climb, slide, wall-run, and grapple through the
environment based on movement context near traversal geometry, **so that** I experience fluid
movement that rewards spatial awareness and momentum.

## US-13.17.3 Swimming and Underwater Exploration
**As a** player, **I want** water volumes to transition my movement to swimming and diving with
an oxygen meter and underwater visual effects, **so that** aquatic environments provide distinct
traversal challenges.
