# User Stories -- Building and Survival (13.14)

## Building Placement

### US-13.14.1 Snap-Based Building Placement
**As a** player, **I want** to place modular building pieces that snap to valid attachment
points with visual feedback, **so that** I can construct structures intuitively without
manual alignment.

### US-13.14.4 Structural Integrity and Building Upgrades
**As a** player, **I want** buildings to have structural integrity that propagates from
foundations and to upgrade pieces through material tiers, **so that** my construction
choices have meaningful consequences.

## Player Housing

### US-13.14.5a Own a Housing Plot

**As a** player, **I want** to claim a housing plot with visitor permissions, **so that** I
have a persistent personal space that I control.

### US-13.14.5b Decorate My Home with Furniture

**As a** player, **I want** to place and arrange furniture inside my home, **so that** I
can personalize my living space.

### US-13.14.5c Benefit from Functional Furniture

**As a** player, **I want** beds to set respawn, chests to extend storage, and stations to
enable crafting, **so that** furnishing my home provides gameplay advantages.

## Survival Systems

### US-13.14.6a Manage Hunger and Thirst

**As a** player, **I want** to eat and drink to maintain hunger and thirst meters, **so
that** I face resource decisions about food and water.

### US-13.14.6b Stay Warm in Harsh Conditions

**As a** player, **I want** clothing, shelter, and fire to protect against cold, **so
that** I must prepare before exploring dangerous biomes.

### US-13.14.6c Manage Stamina During Actions

**As a** player, **I want** stamina to deplete from exertion and recover during rest, **so
that** I must pace my physical activity.

### US-13.14.6d Suffer Penalties from Neglected Vitals

**As a** player, **I want** critically low vitals to impose debuffs, **so that** ignoring
survival needs has meaningful consequences.

## Resource Gathering

### US-13.14.7a Identify Resource Nodes

**As a** player, **I want** resource nodes with clear types and tool requirements, **so
that** I know what to gather and which tool to use.

### US-13.14.7b Gather Resources from Nodes

**As a** player, **I want** to gather resources through an animated interaction that
deposits items in my inventory, **so that** gathering feels satisfying and rewarding.

### US-13.14.7c Find Procedurally Distributed Nodes

**As a** player, **I want** resource nodes distributed across the world by biome, **so
that** exploration is rewarded with new gathering opportunities.

## Farming

### US-13.14.8 Farming and Crop Growth
**As a** designer, **I want** to configure crop growth stages, soil modifiers, and seasonal
constraints through gameplay databases, **so that** I can balance the agricultural economy
without code changes.

## Animal Husbandry

### US-13.14.9a Care for Animals to Boost Production

**As a** player, **I want** feeding and caring for animals to increase their resource
production, **so that** investment in animal welfare pays off.

### US-13.14.9b House Animals in Dedicated Structures

**As a** player, **I want** to build coops, barns, and stables for my animals, **so that**
each species has appropriate housing.

### US-13.14.9c Breed Animals for Better Traits

**As a** player, **I want** to breed animals and see offspring inherit parental traits,
**so that** I can selectively improve my livestock.
